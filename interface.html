LIST OF TOP 10 MUSUMS IN INDIA

  1. Natioanl Musuem, New Delhi

    2. This Musuem is located in heart of the capital, it is one of the largest musuem in India. This musuem has an extensive collection of artifacts from across the India, including relics from the Indus valley civilization, Mughal-era paintings, and ancient manuscripts.This musuem has nearly 17,000 paintings, representing all important styles of Indian miniatures.

    Location: Janpath, New Delhi, India

    Plan Your Visit:

    Timings: Tue-Sun(10:00 AM to 6:00 PM)

    (Closesd on Mondays and National Holidays)

    Entry Fee: Adults: 50 Rupees

    Foreign Nationals: 500 Rupees

    Students up to class 12th: Fee entry(With Id-cards)





  2. Indian Musuem, Kolkata

    3. This musuem is the earliest and the largest multipurpose Museum not only in the Indian subcontinent but also in the Asia-pacific region of the world founded in 1814. It has rare collections of antiques, armour and ornaments, fossils, skeletons, mummies and Mughal paintings.It has six sections comprising of thirty fivr galleries of cultural and scientific artifacts.

    Location: 27, Jawaharlal Nehru Rd, Fire Brigade Head Quarter, New Market Area, Dharmatala, Taltala, Kolkata, West Bengal 700016

    Plan Your Visit:

    Timings: Tue-Sun(10:00 AM to 6:30 PM)

    (Closesd on Mondays and National Holidays)

    Entry Fee: Adults: 50 Rupees

    Foreign Nationals: 500 Rupees

    Students up to class 12th: 20 Rupees
